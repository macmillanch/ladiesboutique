Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order? expand_more
Once your order ships, we'll send a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also find it under 'My Orders' in your profile.
What is your return policy? expand_more
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and tracking tags attached.
Do you ship internationally? expand_more
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on location.
Can I change my payment method? expand_more
For security reasons, we cannot change payment methods once an order is placed. Please contact support to cancel and re-order.
